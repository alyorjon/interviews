# 70 Eng Qiyin Intervyu Savollari va Javoblari
# 70 Toughest Interview Questions and Answers

> **Bilingual Guide | Ikki tilli qo'llanma** — English 🇬🇧 + O'zbek 🇺🇿  
> Har bir javobda real hayotiy misol keltirilgan.  
> Each answer includes a real-life example.

## 1. Tell me about yourself. / O'zingiz haqingizda gapiring.

**EN:** Start with your background, then highlight relevant experience, and finish with what you're passionate about.

> *"I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and 3+ years of experience as a Python backend developer. I've built payment systems, ERP integrations, and Telegram bots. I'm passionate about clean architecture and solving real business problems through automation."*

**UZB:** Avval ta'lim fonini, keyin tajribangizni, so'ngra qiziqishlaringizni aytib bering.

> *"Men kompyuter fanlari bo'yicha bakalavr darajasiga ega bo'lib, 3 yildan ortiq Python backend dasturchi sifatida ishlayman. To'lov tizimlari, ERP integratsiyalari va Telegram botlar yaratganman. Toza arxitektura va avtomatlashtirish orqali biznes muammolarini hal qilish mening qiziqishimdir."*

---

## 2. Why should we hire you? / Nima uchun aynan sizni ishga olishimiz kerak?

**EN:** Focus on your unique value — technical skills + soft skills + proven results.

> *"I bring a rare combination of backend development skills (FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis) and hands-on SAP B1 ERP experience. I've delivered a multi-database credit scoring system serving 500+ clients. I don't just write code — I understand business processes."*

**UZB:** O'zingizning noyob qiymatinglizga e'tibor qarating — texnik ko'nikmalar + yumshoq ko'nikmalar + natijalar.

> *"Men backend ko'nikmalari (FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis) va SAP B1 ERP tajribasini birlashtiraman. 500+ mijozga xizmat qiluvchi ko'p bazali kredit ballashtirish tizimini yetkazib berdim. Men faqat kod yozib qolmay, biznes jarayonlarni ham tushunaman."*

---

## 3. What's your greatest strength? / Eng katta kuchli tomoningiz nima?

**EN:** Choose a strength directly relevant to the role and back it with an example.

> *"Adaptability. When our team had to migrate from a monolith to microservices in 2 months, I quickly learned Docker, set up CI/CD pipelines, and guided junior developers — we delivered on time."*

**UZB:** Lavozimga bevosita aloqador kuchli tomoningizni tanlang va uni misol bilan tasdiqlang.

> *"Moslashuvchanlik. Jamoamiz 2 oyda monolitdan mikroxizmatlarga o'tishi kerak bo'lganda, Docker o'rgandim, CI/CD sozladim va yosh dasturchilarni yo'naltirdim — muddatida yetkazib berdik."*

---

## 4. What's your greatest weakness? / Eng katta zaif tomoningiz nima?

**EN:** Be honest but show growth. Pick a real weakness you've actively improved.

> *"I used to take on too many tasks myself instead of delegating. I've worked on this by creating task templates and trusting teammates with clear ownership — now our team velocity has improved."*

**UZB:** Rostini ayting, lekin rivojlanishingizni ko'rsating. Ishlayotgan zaif tomoningizni tanlang.

> *"Avval vazifalarni o'zimga olish odatim bor edi. Vazifa shablonlari yaratib, jamoaga aniq mas'uliyat topshirishni o'rgandim — hozir jamoamiz samaradorligi oshdi."*

---

## 5. Can you describe a challenging situation you faced at work and how you handled it? / Ishda duch kelgan qiyin vaziyatni va uni qanday hal qilganingizni aytib bering.

**EN:** Use the **STAR** method — Situation, Task, Action, Result.

> *"Our production database went down 2 hours before a major client demo. I quickly identified a misconfigured connection pool, rolled back the config, and set up a read replica as backup — demo went smoothly."*

**UZB:** **STAR** usulidan foydalaning — Vaziyat, Vazifa, Harakat, Natija.

> *"Muhim mijoz namoyishi oldidan 2 soat oldin ishlab chiqarish bazamiz ishlamay qoldi. Noto'g'ri sozlangan ulanish havzasini aniqlab, konfigni orqaga qaytardim va zaxira sifatida o'qish replikasini o'rnatdim — namoyish muammosiz o'tdi."*

---

## 6. Where do you see yourself in five years? / O'zingizni besh yildan keyin qanday ko'rasiz?

**EN:** Show ambition aligned with the company's growth.

> *"I see myself as a senior backend architect, leading system design decisions and mentoring a team. I want to specialize deeper in distributed systems and real-time data pipelines."*

**UZB:** Kompaniya o'sishi bilan uyg'un ambitsiyangizni ko'rsating.

> *"O'zimni senior backend arxitektori sifatida ko'raman — tizim dizayni bo'yicha qarorlar qabul qilib, jamoaga rahbarlik qilaman. Taqsimlangan tizimlar va real vaqtli ma'lumotlar yo'nalishida chuqurroq ixtisoslashmoqchiman."*

## 7. Why did you leave your last job? / Nima uchun oxirgi ishingizdan ketdingiz?

**EN:** Be positive and forward-looking. Never badmouth your previous employer.

> *"I grew a lot in that role, but I reached a ceiling in terms of technical challenges. I'm looking for an environment where I can work on more complex systems and have greater impact."*

**UZB:** Ijobiy va oldinga qarab gapiring. Oldingi ish beruvchingizni hech qachon yomonlamang.

> *"U yerda ko'p narsani o'rgandim, lekin texnik jihatdan potentsialim to'lib qoldi. Ko'proq murakkab tizimlarda ishlash va katta ta'sir ko'rsatish imkoniyatini izlamoqdaman."*

## 8. Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned from it. / Muvaffaqiyatsizligingiz va undan nimani o'rganganingiz haqida gapiring.

**EN:** Show accountability and learning, not blame-shifting.

> *"I once underestimated the complexity of a database migration and caused 3 hours of downtime. I learned to always create a rollback plan, test on staging first, and communicate risk to stakeholders in advance."*

**UZB:** Aybni boshqaga yuklamasdan, mas'uliyat va o'rganishni ko'rsating.

> *"Bir marta ma'lumotlar bazasini ko'chirishning murakkabligini kam baholadim va 3 soatlik uzilishga sabab bo'ldim. Har doim orqaga qaytish rejasi tuzishni, avval test muhitida sinashni va xatarlar haqida oldindan xabardor qilishni o'rgandim."*

## 9. How do you handle stress or pressure? / Stress va bosimni qanday boshqarasiz?

**EN:** Show concrete strategies, not just "I stay calm."

> *"I break down the problem into smaller tasks, prioritize by impact, and communicate blockers early. During a critical API outage, this approach helped me stay focused and resolve the issue in under 90 minutes."*

**UZB:** Faqat "xotirjam qolaman" emas, aniq strategiyalarni ko'rsating.

> *"Muammoni kichik vazifalarga bo'laman, ta'sir bo'yicha ustuvorlik qilaman va to'siqlar haqida erta xabar beraman. Kritik API uzilishi paytida bu yondashuv 90 daqiqadan kam vaqtda muammoni hal qilishga yordam berdi."*

## 10. Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult coworker. / Muammoli hamkor bilan ishlagan vaziyatingizni tasvirlab bering.

**EN:** Show maturity and professionalism, not conflict.

> *"A colleague consistently missed deadlines affecting my work. I scheduled a private 1:1, understood their blockers (overload), and we jointly restructured task assignments — collaboration improved significantly."*

**UZB:** Ziddiyat emas, yetuklik va professionallikni ko'rsating.

> *"Bir hamkasbim doimo muddatlarni o'tkazib yuborar, bu ishimga ta'sir qilardi. Xususiy suhbat o'tkazib, ularning to'siqlarini (haddan tashqari yuklanish) tushundim va vazifalarni birgalikda qayta tarqatdik — hamkorlik sezilarli darajada yaxshilandi."*

## 11. What's your leadership style? / Rahbarlik uslubingiz qanday?

**EN:** Be specific — not just "collaborative."

> *"Collaborative and outcome-driven. I set clear goals, give autonomy, but hold weekly syncs to unblock issues. When I led the SAP B1 implementation, this style helped us finish 2 months ahead of schedule."*

**UZB:** Aniq bo'ling — faqat "hamkorlikchi" emas.

> *"Hamkorlikka va natijalarga yo'naltirilgan. Aniq maqsadlar qo'yaman, mustaqillik beraman, lekin haftalik sinxronizatsiyalar o'tkazaman. SAP B1 joriy etishda bu uslub bizga 2 oy muddatdan oldin tugatishga yordam berdi."*

## 12. How do you stay updated with industry trends and developments? / Soha yangiliklari va tendentsiyalardan qanday xabardor bo'lasiz?

## 14. Why do you want to work here? / Nima uchun bu yerda ishlashni xohlaysiz?

> *"Your engineering culture values developer autonomy and clean code — I saw this in your open-source contributions. I want to grow in an environment that treats backend architecture as a first-class concern."*

## 15. Can you provide an example of a time you had to adapt to a change at work? / Ishda o'zgarishga moslashgan misolingizni keltiring.

> *"When management decided to switch our ORM from raw SQL to SQLAlchemy mid-project, I created a migration guide, converted the first 5 models as examples, and ran a short team workshop — we adapted in 3 days."*

**UZB:** O'zgarish = imkoniyat. Uni qanday qabul qilganingizni ko'rsating.

> *"Menejment loyiha o'rtasida oddiy SQL'dan SQLAlchemy ORM'ga o'tishga qaror qilganida, ko'chirish qo'llanmasini yaratdim, dastlabki 5 moddelni misol sifatida o'zgartirdim va qisqa jamoa sessiyasi o'tkazdim — 3 kunda moslashdik."*

## 16. What motivates you in your career? / Karerrangizda sizni nima rag'batlantiradi?

**EN:** Be genuine — interviewers spot generic answers immediately.

> *"Seeing a system I built handle real load in production. When our payment notification bot processed 10,000 transactions in a day without issues, that was deeply satisfying."*

**UZB:** Chin bo'ling — suhbatdoshlar umumiy javoblarni darhol bilib oladi.

> *"O'zim qurgan tizimning ishlab chiqarishda haqiqiy yukni ko'tarishini ko'rish. To'lov bildirishnomalar botimiz bir kunda 10,000 ta tranzaksiyani muammosiz qayta ishlagan kuni bu juda qoniqarli bo'ldi."*

## 17. Describe a project where you had to meet tight deadlines. / Qattiq muddatga rioya qilish zarur bo'lgan loyiha haqida gapiring.

**EN:** Use STAR. Emphasize planning and communication, not just "working hard."

> *"For a government tender system, we had a strict 6-week deadline. I broke it into 2-week sprints, deployed a CI/CD pipeline from day 1, and held daily standups. We launched 3 days early."*

**UZB:** STAR usulini qo'llang. Faqat "qattiq ishlash" emas, rejalashtirish va muloqotni ta'kidlang.

> *"Davlat tender tizimi uchun qat'iy 6 haftalik muddatimiz bor edi. Uni 2 haftalik sprintlarga bo'ldim, 1-kundan CI/CD yo'nalishini o'rnatdim va kunlik standaplar o'tkazdim. 3 kun avval ishga tushirdik."*

## 18. What is your preferred work style: independently or in a team? / Qanday ish uslubini afzal ko'rasiz: mustaqil yoki jamoada?

**EN:** Show flexibility — both have value in different contexts.

> *"I thrive in both. Deep backend work — like optimizing queries or designing schemas — needs focused solo time. But system design and API contracts benefit enormously from team discussion."*

**UZB:** Moslashuvchanlikni ko'rsating — har ikkalasi turli kontekstlarda qimmatlidir.

> *"Ikkalasida ham samarali ishlayman. So'rovlarni optimallashtirish yoki sxema dizayni kabi chuqur backend ishi uchun individual diqqat kerak. Lekin tizim dizayni va API shartnomalar jamoaviy muhokamadan katta foyda ko'radi."*

## 19. How do you handle constructive criticism? / Konstruktiv tanqidni qanday qabul qilasiz?

**EN:** Show openness and action, not defensiveness.

> *"I welcome it. During a code review, a senior developer pointed out my API lacked proper error handling. I didn't just fix it — I created a shared error handling module used across the whole project."*

**UZB:** Mudofaakorlik emas, ochiqlik va harakatni ko'rsating.

> *"Uni xush qabul qilaman. Kod ko'rib chiqishda, senior dasturchi mening API'imda to'g'ri xato boshqaruvi yo'qligini ko'rsatdi. Faqat tuzatib qolmay — butun loyihada ishlatiladigan umumiy xato boshqaruv modulini yaratdim."*

Men ikkalasini ham bajardim: murakkab ko'p bazali arxitekturalar qurdim VA loyiha rahbari sifatida SAP B1 ERP joriy etishni boshqardim. Bu ikki tomonlama ko'rinish meni biznes natijalarida fikrlaydigan dasturchi qiladi."*

## 70. Do you have any questions for us? / Bizga savollaringiz bormi?

**EN:** Always have 2–3 smart questions ready. It shows genuine interest and preparation.

> *"1. What does the onboarding process look like for senior engineers?*  
> *2. What's the biggest technical challenge the backend team is currently facing?*  
> *3. How do you measure engineering success — velocity, quality, or impact?"*

**UZB:** Har doim 2–3 ta aqlli savolni tayyor turing. Bu haqiqiy qiziqish va tayyorgarlikni ko'rsatadi.

> *"1. Senior muhandislar uchun onboarding jarayoni qanday ko'rinadi?*  
> *2. Backend jamoasi hozir duch kelayotgan eng katta texnik muammo nima?*  
> *3. Muhandislik muvaffaqiyatini qanday o'lchaybsiz — tezlik, sifat yoki ta'sir?"*

## 📌 STAR Usuli / STAR Method

Behavioral savollarga javob berishda **STAR** usulidan foydalaning:

| Harf | Ma'no (UZB) | Meaning (EN) |
|------|------------|--------------|
| **S** | Vaziyat — kontekstni tushuntiring | Situation — set the context |
| **T** | Vazifa — sizning rolингизни aniqlang | Task — define your role |
| **A** | Harakat — nima qilganingizni batafsil ayting | Action — detail what YOU did |
| **R** | Natija — o'lchanadigan natijani ko'rsating | Result — show measurable outcome |
